Frequently asked questions

My widget shows zeros or "Open app to log in"

Open the Horas app and log in with your Dreaming Spanish account, then tap Sync. Widgets read the data the app saves, so they need one successful sync first. If a widget still looks stale, remove it from your home screen and add it again.

The widget says "Session expired"

Dreaming Spanish sessions expire from time to time. Open the app and it will prompt you to log in again — your history and streak are unaffected.

My Apple Watch isn't syncing

Open the watch app while your iPhone is nearby — it pulls the latest data from the phone automatically. If it stays empty: make sure the Horas watch app and the iPhone app are both up to date (delete and reinstall the watch app from the Watch app on your iPhone if needed), then open Horas on the iPhone, tap Sync, and reopen the watch app.

How do I sync between iPhone and iPad?

Turn on iCloud sync in the app's settings on both devices. Data moves through your personal iCloud account only — it never touches our servers, because we don't have any.

How often do widgets refresh?

Automatically about every 30 minutes (15 with iCloud sync on), and instantly whenever you open the app or tap the sync button on a widget. iOS may slow refreshes down in Low Power Mode.

Does Horas work without a Dreaming Spanish account?

You need a Dreaming Spanish account for your input-hours data. Horas is an independent companion app — it displays and tracks your progress but is not affiliated with Dreaming Spanish.

What data do you collect?

None. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts with us. See the privacy policy for the full picture.
